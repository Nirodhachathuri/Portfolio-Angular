SCIENCE E-GLOSSARY

A science words dictionary is developed here to search words with meanings in subjects such as Physics, Chemistry, Botany, and Zoology, separately. This application is developed with the Java Programming Language and My SQL database server. Main features are to choose the word category, type the word in text area and to give the English word with its Sinhala meaning. For some words, it gives Sinhala meaning and a short description of the word as well.
